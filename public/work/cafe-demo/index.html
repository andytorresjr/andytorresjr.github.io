Our Story

Small café.
Big leaf energy.

Verde Matcha Café is a small-batch tea bar in Riverside, Texas — built around one stubborn idea: that good matcha shouldn't be a luxury reserved for tasting menus and ceremonies.

We import ceremonial-grade matcha and whisk every cup by hand with a bamboo chasen. We pair it with Tex-Mex traditions like the concha, kawaii merch from Miffy to Totoro, and the kind of cozy, quiet room you want to spend a Saturday morning in.

Tradition. Trend. Tex-Mex. All in one cup.
