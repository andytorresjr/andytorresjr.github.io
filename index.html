About Me

I am currently an electrical and computer engineering(ECE) student at the University of Texas at Austin. I was born and raised in Laredo, Texas where I attended Sanchez Ochoa Elementary school before transferring to Matias de Llano Elementary School for grades 1st through 5th. For middle school, I attended Trautmann Middle School for grades 6th through 8th. After middle school, I chose to attend United High School for their engineering magnet program as well as the various amount of good math teachers.


Roots

My mother, Patricia Torres, was born in a small town named El Salto del Agua located in San Luis. She lived there for most of her life where she attended school and relocated to San Luis Potosi for high school. She graduated from high high school and moved to the United States where she searched for a job to settle herself in. While job searching, she ran into my father in Austin, Texas and they kicked it off well before they fell in love and moved in together at my father's hometown house with his mother. Once my mom began to live there, she was able to find sustainable jobs where made a name for herself in the accounting industry. After several years, my mother and father had my oldest sister in 1997, my younger sister in 2002 and then finally having me in 2004.
