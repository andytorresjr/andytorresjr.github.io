Resources

Paul's Online Math Notes- I like to think of Paul's notes as the closest thing to perfect as far as notetaking goes. I have entire sections on my notebooks of his online notes that I like to use as introductory material
HyperMath- HyperMath is another cool math resource that I used as introductory material, however, I probably didn't use HyperMath half as much as I use Paul's notes simply because of how straightforward Paul's notes are.
HyperPhysics- This site CARRIED me during physics (both mechanical and quantum/EM/semiconductor). Everything you need to know about physics is definitely here along with conceptual explanations with their derived equations.

These are just a few of the resources I have used and continue to use throughout my engineering journey. I will be adding more resources as I find them and as I continue to learn more about engineering.
